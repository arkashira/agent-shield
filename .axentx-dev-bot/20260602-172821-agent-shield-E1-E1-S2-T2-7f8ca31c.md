5. **Logging** – Each trip logs a structured message containing: tool‑key, final failure count, timestamp, and error details.  
6. **Concurrency safety** – Concurrent accesses (multiple threads invoking the same or different keys) never corrupt the internal `HashMap` (no lost updates, no panics).  
7. **Key isolation** – Failure counts, state, and timers are maintained **independently** per tool‑key; a trip on key *A* does not affect key *B*.
